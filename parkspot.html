Finding Car parking spot in office

Project Concept Name

ParkSpot App

Project Summary

The concept behind the app is to help employee or the security to find a car parking at office buildings. The technology used is, RFID tags that are installed at every parking spot with IR proximity sensor, Microcontroller chip(MCU) and RFID reader. The car slot availability is tracked and sent to the server. The app displays the availability of parking spot based on the data the server passes to the front end app. The employee can book the parking spot andn park the car. The whole project is about the technology and the experience how user books the parking spot.

Below, I have posted the figma prototype which shows the design of various UX Flows. The UX flows for employee and security have been simulated for demo purpose.
