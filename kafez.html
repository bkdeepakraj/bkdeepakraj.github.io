Kafez Coffee Shop

UX Design - Visual Design

Project Summary

Kafez is one of the biggest coffee beans producers in the world. They sell coffee beans, coffee brewing machines and other coffee related products. Kafez has retail outlets selling coffee to customers with their customizable 15 different varieties of coffee to customers. Kafez also wants to sell their coffee related products and appetizers present inside the store and included in the app for ordering.

The UX Challenge

Kafez wanted to go digital and need an app for their retail outlets for taking coffee orders from customers. So that the waiting time is reduced and ordering coffee is simplified and increase their revenue. Kafez has a plan to invest in the app and approaches companies to create the app with best user experience.

The UX Approach

UX Strategy and Proto-Persona were created as the starting point to design the experience. And User Journey was created to know the tasks and user behaviors while using the app. How the user feels, user thinks during and what he does while performing the tasks.

Visual Design

The branding, style guide, icons and layout were created using Figma.
