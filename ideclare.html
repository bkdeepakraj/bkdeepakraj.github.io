Portfolio, IDeclare Hybrid(IOS and Android) Mobile App

Project Summary

The IDeclare App is used by travelers who enter Dubai airport to declare the items while entering or leaving the country. The Declaration form is simplified for all the users. The travelers can declare the form as registered user or guest user. The registered user country address and mobile number is stored in the database. Both the registered user and guest user after declaring the form, a QR Code is generated.

When the traveler enters the Dubai airport, the Dubai customs officer scans the QR Code to make the airport checking for goods very simple and hassle free. If the goods the traveler carry are within permitted limits the traveler is allowed to go through green channel. If not within permitted limits they have to go through red channel. The app prohibits of bringing illegal goods inside the country, by allowing travelers to select legal goods and within permissible limits (number of items or goods weight) that is allowed by Dubai Customs. After creating the declaration form a QR code is generated. While entering or leaving the airport the QR Code is scanned by the Customs officer to verify the goods.

The UX Challenge

Due to lack of background research of users I created proto-persona. The behaviors and goals described in the proto-persona and the requirement definition from the client were consider as the starting point for design.

The UX Approach

Stakeholders were informed about the UX Strategy and the goals that were set in proto-persona. Based on the design decisions, I created the Information Architecture, Task flow, and Wireframes.
