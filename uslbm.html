USLBM Hybrid Mobile App

A supply app for contractor, builder, or remodeler to track jobsite deliveries, check billing information and more

UX/UI Visual Design

Project Summary

USLBM is a leading distributor of specialty building materials in the United States. The app can track job site deliveries, Check Billing information and check latest promotions and events.

The app is used by the contractors and Engineers(Users). Currently 13 contractors who have signed up with USLBM use to Track deliveries with realâ€“time GPS updates and send message to Engineer with status updates about the delivery. The app also is used to check the billing information like invoice and monthly statements. The app updates latest promotions and events to the users

The UX Challenge

The client did not have written documentation on the requirement. During the initial stages of design, every week the requirement for each functionality is discussed in the meeting with the team. Initially User Research was not done. Since the user were near the client, he was able to test the app with contractors and engineers when needed to get feedback.

The UX Approach

Through design iteration the screens were designed for each functionality. The goals of the user were prioritized and the design changes if relevant were accommodated based on the client feedback who closely tested the app with users (contractors and engineers). The technical constraints were considered for designing the screens. Apache Cordova(single base code for IOS and Android) mobile development framework was used to create the app.

Stakeholders

Consultant from USLBM(client) and Wipro Business Analyst

Visual Design

The client had a branding in place. The colors and typography were decided. During initial stages the branding was used, later as the project progressed the style guide was refined. All the delivery status icons were designed by me.

Mobile and Tablet Designs

The app was designed for both Mobile and Tablet

Home and Promotions Screen

Initial set of screens showing promotions and events.

Track Deliveries, Delivery Information and Billing Information screens

User can track deliveries, see delivery information and billing information(invoices).

Visual Design - Icons.

Delivery information status icons were created to show the status of the order. The icons were exported to mdpi 1x, xhdpi 2x and xxhdpi 3x for IOS and Android
