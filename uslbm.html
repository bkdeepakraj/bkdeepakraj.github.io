USLBM Mobile and Tablet App

UX Design - UI Design

Project Background

USLBM is a leading distributor of specialty building materials in the United States. The app can track job site deliveries, Check Billing information and check latest promotions and events.

Role

UX/UI Designer

Tools

Photoshop, Adobe XD, Mockflow, Illustrator

Date

2016-2018

App Store Link

USLBM-Hines App

Target Audience and Problem the product solves

The app is used by the contractors and Engineers(Users). Currently 13 contractors who have signed up with USLBM use to Track deliveries with realâ€“time GPS updates and send message to Engineer with status updates about the delivery. The app also is used to check the billing information like invoice and monthly statements. The app updates latest promotions and events to the users.

The business challenges the team wanted to solve with the project

The main challenge is to make the users to adopt the app to check their delivery status, billing information like invoice and monthly statements. And to promote the events to the users. The app needs to be built responsive and it can be accessed using mobile, tablet and desktop.

My role and people I worked in the project

My role was to create User Experience for the USLBM App. I created Persona, Task Flow, Information architecture, Wireframes and Visual Design.

Interacted with Client, Wipro Business Analyst and Wipro Developers. Refined the requirement as the project progressed to align both business strategy and UX strategy

Design Process

  1. Empathize - User Interview
  2. Define - User Persona, User Journey Mapping
  3. Ideate - Sketches, Sitemap.
  4. Design/Build - Prototype(Visual Design)

1.Empathize - User Interviews

Conducted User Interviews with contractors and Engineers as part of User Research. The users wanted to use the app for tracking deliveries, able to view invoices and monthly statements.

2.Define - Persona, User Journey

Persona

After getting insights from user research I had a clear understanding of who my target user will be and created persona.

I referred to them throughout the entire product development process. For each persona I identified the key tasks as well as their emotional state at each stage in the journey.

User Journey

Sketches
