Building material supplier App

Project Name

USLBM

Project Tagline

An app to track jobsite deliveries, check your billing information, sign up for events and promotions for building products distributors USLBM.

Project Summary

US LBM is a leading distributor of specialty building materials in the United States. They needed a app for contractors and builders to track jobsite deliveries, check billing information, sign up for events and promotions. My role as UX Designer is to create the UX workflow for tracking deliveries, display billing information, create signup form for events and promotions.

Client Name

USLBM Holdings LLC.

Timeframe

March 2016 to April 2018, Wipro (Offsite)

My Major Tasks & Responsibilities

My task was to understand the requirements for tracking real-time GPS information and to display real-time data, the billing information that has to be displayed in the app and easy way to view and retrieve the information.

Platforms

Hybrid Mobile App (one single code for Android and IOS)

Key Performance Metrics

Users Engagement with App, Accuracy of jobsite delivery and Ease of use.

Team Members & Collaborators

Business Analyst(SME's), Visual Designer, UI Developer, Client, Project Manager

Target Audience and Problem the product solves

The app is used by the contractors and engineers(Users). Currently 10 building suppliers who have signed up with USLBM. The contractors and engineers(Users) use to Track deliveries with real–time GPS updates and send message to Engineer with status updates about the delivery by the truck drivers. The app also is used to check the billing information like invoice and monthly statements. The app updates latest promotions and events to the users. The app features are,

Track Jobsite Deliveries
  1. Track deliveries with real–time GPS updates
  2. View photos of the delivered order
  3. Quickly send text messages with delivery status to your GC or Sub
  4. Report any issues with your delivery
  5. Review material list for accuracy
  6. Check recent deliveries and search delivery history
Check Billing Information
  1. View invoice copies and your monthly statements
  2. Access billing reports to help manage your account.
Check Our Latest Promotions and Events
  1. Browse and share current promotions and events
  2. Sign up for our events at a tap of a button

Design Process

  1. Empathize - User Interview
  2. Define - User Persona, User Journey Mapping
  3. Ideate - Sketches, Sitemap.
  4. Design/Build - Prototype(Visual Design)

1.Empathize - User Interviews

Conducted User Interviews with contractors and Engineers as part of User Research. The users wanted to use the app for tracking deliveries, able to view invoices and monthly statements.

2.Define - Persona, User Journey

Persona

After getting insights from user research I had a clear understanding of who my target user will be and created persona.

I referred to them throughout the entire product development process. For each persona I identified the key tasks as well as their emotional state at each stage in the journey.
