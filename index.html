About me

I am Deepak Madhana Raj, currently working as UX Designer and I am creative and passionate about my work. I have 11+ years experience in UX Design, 3 years in creating Low and High Fiedility prototypes for UX Research Team for Testing using HTML, CSS and Intermediate Javascript, 2 years in creating E-learning courses and 3 Years as Web Designer.

What I do as UX Designer? I follow Design Thinking (Empathize, Define, Ideate, Prototype and Testing) Process for my UX projects. I can create Product Idea Generation or Product Story, User Persona, User Journey, User Stories, Sitemap (IA), Wireflows (Wireframes and Task flows) and Visual Design. I use Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, XD, Figma, Mockflow, Bootstrap 4 and Dreamweaver to create my UX Artifacts.

What I have not done as UX Designer? User Research and Usability Testing with real users. I have collaborated with User Reseachers during prototype testing. I can review and understand the documents provided by the User Research team.

I am looking for job in a company which has design as a core domain with growth aspects within the company.

My hobbies. I like to paint, sketch on paper & digital devices, listen to music and watch movies. I love sports. I play cricket, table tennis, badminton, baseball, football, and love skating on my skateboard.
