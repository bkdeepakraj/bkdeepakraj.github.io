HP Print Design: Workflows for Printer Control Panel

HP's division Print Design create and handle workflows for HP Printers(control panel - touch screen). The team consist of UX Designers, Pattern team, IA Team and Firmware team. I joined the Print Design Team in HP as UX Designer. I was assigned to create UX workflow for the Tools section. Under Tools menu the workflow for Reports, Maintenance, Troubleshooting and Service was created and handed over to the firmware team.

Created UX Workflows in Axure RP based on requirement from tech lead and Firmware lead, referred IA document and HP Print Patterns
