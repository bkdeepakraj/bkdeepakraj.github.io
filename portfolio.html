My best projects

Project: IDeclare Mobile App, Dubai customs.

Wipro, 2017-2018

The IDeclare App is used by travelers who enter Dubai airport to declare the items while entering the country. The Declaration form is simplified for all the users. The travelers can declare the form as registered user or guest user. The registered user country address and mobile number is stored in the database. Both the registered user and guest user after declaring the form, a QR Code is generated. When the traveler enters the Dubai airport, the Dubai customs officer scans the QR Code to make the airport checking for goods very simple and hassle free. If the goods the traveler carry are within permitted limits the traveler is allowed to go through green channel. If not within permitted limits they have to go through red channel. The app prohibits of bringing illeagl goods inside the country, by selecting legal goods that is allowed by Dubai Customs.
