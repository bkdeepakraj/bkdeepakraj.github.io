HP Print Design: Workflows for Printer Control Panel

HP's division Print Design create and handle workflows for HP Printers(control panel - touch screen). The team consist of UX Designers, Pattern team, IA Team and Firmware team. I joined the Print Design Team in HP as UX Designer. I was assigned to create UX workflow for the Tools section. Under Tools menu the workflow for Reports, Maintenance, Troubleshooting and Service was created and handed over to the firmware team.

Created UX Workflows in Axure RP based on requirement from tech lead and Firmware lead, referred IA document and HP Print Patterns

This Project is under Non Disclosure Agreement

Lombard Asset Management, E-sign

Lombard E-sign project is part of Asset Management in Lombard. Assets like Vehicles, Agricultural, Marine, Technology, and others assets are financed to the customers or companies. During the Quote & finance for the asset, Lombard agent generally follow manual process to get the documents verified and signed (wet-sign) by customers. Lombard E-sign is a process where all the documents are verified and signed by customers digitally.

Used Design thinking process to create UX Strategy, Empathy Map, Persona, Interaction Design, Task Flow, IA, Wireframes and Visual Design
